Accessibility Statement

Last updated: June 2026

Sami's is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We continually improve the user experience for everyone and apply relevant accessibility standards.

Conformance Status

This website targets conformance with WCAG 2.1 Level AA (Web Content Accessibility Guidelines). We believe it is substantially conformant, with ongoing improvements underway.

What We've Done

  • Semantic HTML5 landmarks (<header>, <main>, <nav>, <footer>)
  • Skip-to-content link at the top of every page
  • All images have descriptive alt text; decorative images are marked alt=""
  • Keyboard-navigable navigation with visible focus indicators
  • Sufficient color contrast ratios (minimum 4.5:1 for body text)
  • ARIA labels on interactive controls and dialogs
  • Responsive design that works down to 320px viewport width
  • Reduced-motion support via prefers-reduced-motion media query
  • Cookie consent dialog is keyboard-accessible and focus-managed

Known Limitations

Embedded Google Maps content may have accessibility limitations that are outside our direct control. We provide address and phone information as alternatives.
